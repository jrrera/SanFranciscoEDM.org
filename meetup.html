PLUR



We've been around since November 2012; our #1 goal has been community, rather than promotion. As such, we also do barhopping, potlucks, kandi making, and more.


Looking to meet some new people?

You've come to the right place! Although our Meetup has over 1,000 people, we do our best to make it feel like a tight-knit group. All you need to do is bring good vibes and we'll do the rest. If you haven't joined our Meetup.com group yet, I'd highly recommend it.


Looking to help out?

We all love the scene, but bringing people together for dozens of events yearly is tough work, so you'll sometimes see events posted without any social component. That's where you come in!

We're constantly on the lookout for people who love the electronic dance music community and would be willing to help our Meetup out. How? By being a leader in the community; someone who can coordinate an event and help bring people together before and during the show.

Sound interesting? If so, shoot me (Jon) an email through the Meetup.com platform (easy to do once you join the group). :)
